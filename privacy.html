Privacy Policy

Froggy Squat (Chinese name: 蹲蹲哇) — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Froggy Squat is a fitness app that uses your device's motion sensors to count squats and time static squat holds. This policy explains how we handle motion and fitness-related data. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensor data to our servers. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising profiling, or misuse it for any purpose other than operating the app on your device.

Motion & sensor data

The app accesses device motion sensors (such as accelerometer and related motion data) solely to detect squat posture and count repetitions or hold duration. This processing happens on your device only. Raw or derived sensor readings are not uploaded to us, not shared with third parties, and not used for analytics or marketing.

Data we use

  • Motion sensors: Used only for squat detection and counting as described above. Sensor data never leaves your device for our use.
  • Local storage: Training records and app settings are stored on your device only. We cannot access this data.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No account or personal information
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs that send your activity to us
  • No advertising identifiers

In-App Purchases

Premium features use Apple's In-App Purchase. Payment and purchase verification are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Motion data is processed locally as described above and is not transmitted to us.
