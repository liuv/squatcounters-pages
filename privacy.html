Privacy Policy

SquatCounter (蹲蹲哇) — Last updated: March 2025

Overview

SquatCounter is a fitness app that uses your device's motion sensor to count squats and time static squat holds. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

Data We Use

  • Motion sensor: Used only to detect squat posture and count repetitions. Sensor data is processed locally and never leaves your device.
  • Local storage: Training records and app settings are stored on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or personal information
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers

In-App Purchases

Premium features use Apple's In-App Purchase. Payment and purchase verification are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.
