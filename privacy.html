Privacy Policy

Froggy Squat (listed on the App Store and Google Play as Froggy Squat; in-app may show localized names) — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Froggy Squat is a fitness app that uses your device's motion sensor to count squats and time static squat holds. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

Data We Use

  • Motion and fitness (sensor) data: The app reads device motion (e.g. accelerometer) solely to recognize squat posture and wall-sit timing. Processing happens on your device in real time. We do not upload sensor readings to our servers, sell them, use them for advertising, or build profiles about you.
  • Local storage: Training records and app settings are stored on your device only. We do not have remote access to this data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or personal information
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers

Data Retention

We do not operate servers that store your training data. All data created by the app stays on your device until you delete it or remove the app.

  • Training records: Kept in local storage on your device for as long as you keep them. They are not synced to our servers.
  • App settings and preferences: Stored locally (e.g. theme, reminders, challenge progress). Retained until you change them, reset them in the app, or uninstall the app.
  • Premium trial timing: Stored only on your device; removed if you uninstall the app.

How to Delete Your Data (In the App)

You can delete your data without contacting us:

  • Delete one training record: Open History, delete a single entry (e.g. swipe or use the delete control on a row).
  • Delete all training records: Open History and use Clear all / clear-all records to remove every stored workout record at once.
  • Reset app settings: Open Settings and use Reset to defaults to restore appearance and preference options to defaults (this does not remove training records by itself).
  • Remove everything: Uninstalling the app from your device deletes the app’s local database and preferences. We have no remote copy to delete because we do not collect this data on our servers.

If you use premium backup export, any file you saved is under your control—delete the file from your device or cloud storage if you no longer want it.

In-App Purchases

Premium features use in-app purchase on Apple App Store and Google Play. Payment and purchase verification are handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not receive or store any payment card information.
